Unified Payment Notification

One complete payment story, no matter which channel the user sees it on first — email, bell/dashboard, or push.

Writer Jane Radianti Sugianto
Reviewers Rinaldi Limeo
BU Payments · RNG
Created July 2026
🎯

The bet

If every payment notification — email, bell/dashboard, and push — carries the right set of payment variables for its surface, users will be able to trust and act on the notification itself, without opening the dashboard, checking their email, or contacting support to confirm a transaction went through correctly.

Email, Bell/Dashboard, and Push are now live or shipping across all four payment modules — PayIn via Payment Link, PayIn Static VA, PayOut Success, and PayOut Disbursement — with Push still rolling out on mobile only. WhatsApp remains disabled and stays out of scope.

🔍

The problem

  • 📧
    Email notification is incompleteThe PayIn & PayOut email template exists but is missing key payment variables, so users can't fully verify a payment from the email alone.
  • 🔔
    Bell/dashboard notification is incompleteThe in-app notification for PayIn (Static VA) and PayOut is also missing variables that the transaction record already contains.
  • 📱
    Push notification isn't live yetPush exists on mobile only and still needs to reach the same standard as email and bell/dashboard.
  • 🤷
    Users fall back to the dashboardIncomplete notifications on any channel force users to log in and manually check the transaction — increasing support queries.
⚡

What we're building

Three channels — Email, Bell Notification/Dashboard, and Push Notification — each carrying a variable set tailored to that surface, across four payment modules: PayIn via Payment Link, PayIn Static VA, PayOut Success (Single PI / Single PYOR / Bulk PI / Bulk PYOR), and PayOut Disbursement (Single PI / Single PYOR / Bulk PI / Bulk PYOR). Bell/Dashboard is developed for both Web and Mobile. Push is Mobile only. WhatsApp is out of scope everywhere — it's currently disabled and stays that way.

A. Email notification 4 modules · all Live
Applies to every ticket in this group Email must render correctly on Gmail (web and mobile), Outlook, and mobile clients (iOS Mail, Android Gmail) with no broken layout, missing variables, or encoding issues, and must match the approved Hi-Fi design exactly. No changes are made to the WhatsApp template (currently disabled). Template switchover must not break in-flight notifications.
