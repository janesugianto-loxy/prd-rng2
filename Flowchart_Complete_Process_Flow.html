📋 Order & payment status reference

Every event in the order lifecycle, showing what happens to Order Status, Payment Status, the Sales Invoice, and PaperPay In. Rows 4 and 6a are highlighted — these are the cases where SI and PayIn are deleted outright because funds never settled with the merchant.

# Event / trigger Order status Payment status Sales invoice PaperPay In Notes
1 Customer is on the "choose payment method" page Pending Awaiting Payment Not yet created Not yet created No expiry — customer can take as long as needed.
2 Payment completes (CC charged or VA transferred) Pending Paid Created — Paid Created The 30-minute confirm clock starts now — not at order submission.
3 Merchant confirms within 30 min Confirmed Paid Stays Paid Stays on record No auto-confirm — only on explicit merchant action.
4 30 minutes pass with no merchant action Cancelled (auto) Refunded Deleted Deleted System auto-cancels and refunds in full — same outcome for CC and VA.
5 Merchant declines before the customer has paid Cancelled Unpaid Never created Never created Nothing was charged, so nothing to refund.
6a Merchant cancels a paid CC order — before settlement (funds not yet in Active Balance) Cancelled Refunded Deleted Deleted Instant gateway refund — no manual step.
6b Merchant cancels a paid CC order — after settlement into Active Balance Cancelled Paid (unchanged) Stays Paid Stays on record Behaves exactly like a VA cancellation (row 7) — settled charge can't be reversed automatically.
7 Merchant cancels a paid VA order Cancelled Paid (unchanged) Stays Paid Stays on record VA funds are instant to balance — always row 7, never row 6a.
8 Merchant marks a cancelled order "Refunded" after completing manual refund (rows 6b and 7) Cancelled Refunded Reverts to Unpaid Unchanged — stays on record Not deleted — money genuinely arrived and left via a separate manual refund, so it stays for the books.
9 Merchant marks a Confirmed order delivered Done Paid Stays Paid Stays on record Terminal status, set manually — no further changes expected.
